# Vendor Information Security Requirements

Document owner: Third-Party Risk Management  
Effective date: 2023-01-15  
Status: Obsolete - retained for audit history

## 1. Purpose and scope

This archived standard applied to vendors that processed company confidential data or connected to company-managed systems.

## 2. Onboarding evidence

2.1 The vendor shall submit a security attestation within 30 days of onboarding.

## 3. Incident response governance

3.1 The vendor shall maintain an incident response plan within 45 days of onboarding.

## 4. Incident notification

4.1 The vendor shall notify the buyer of any breach within 72 hours.
