# Vendor Information Security Requirements

Document owner: Third-Party Risk Management  
Effective date: 2026-04-01  
Status: Current

## 1. Purpose and scope

This standard applies to all vendors that process company confidential data or connect to company-managed systems. The requirements below are binding unless a written exception is approved by Third-Party Risk Management.

## 2. Onboarding evidence

2.1 The vendor shall submit a security attestation within 30 days of onboarding.

2.2 The attestation shall identify the vendor security contact and describe the control environment used for the contracted service.

## 3. Incident notification

3.1 The vendor shall notify the buyer of any data breach within 72 hours of discovery.

3.2 The notice shall include the incident date, affected service, known data categories, and planned containment actions.

## 4. Incident response governance

4.1 The vendor shall maintain an incident response plan.

4.2 The incident response plan shall identify escalation contacts, decision authority, and communication procedures.

## 5. Security testing

5.1 The vendor shall complete annual penetration testing within 12 months of contract start.

5.2 The vendor shall provide an executive summary of the final penetration test report upon request.

## 6. Vulnerability response

6.1 The vendor shall remediate critical vulnerabilities within 7 days of identification.
